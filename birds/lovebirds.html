Lovebirds are small parrots that are known for their playful and affectionate personalities. They are social birds that form strong bonds with their mates and can also bond with their owners. The page will feature lovebird species, such as the opaline lovebird and the black-masked lovebird. The page will also contain general information about African lovebirds.
