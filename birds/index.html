About Us

The Parrot Ship is a family-owned aviary that started in 2020. It was founded by a seafarer who wanted to share their love for birds with others. Although the aviary is not limited to only a few bird species, The Parrot Ship hopes to be able to expand and accommodate more parrots in the future.
